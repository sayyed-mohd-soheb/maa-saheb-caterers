📖 Our Journey

We started our catering business 13 years ago, initially serving parties, weddings and small gatherings. Over time, our experience helped us grow and bring new ideas into our work.

🙏 Niyaz Initiative

During COVID-19, we expanded our services to distribute food to the poor and needy across slums in Mumbai. Today, this has become a major part of our mission to serve those in need.
