Niyaz — Feeding the Needy

With every order, a portion of our food is set aside as Niyaz — distributing meals to the less fortunate in our community. Your order is not just a meal; it is a small act of charity.

Maa-Shaeb Caterers was founded to bring home-cooked warmth to weddings, gatherings, and events. Every dish is prepared fresh with hand-picked spices and generations-old recipes passed down with love.
